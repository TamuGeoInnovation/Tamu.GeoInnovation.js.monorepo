Behind the Texas A&M Aggie Map

Aggiemap is the official map for Texas A&M University; a collaboration between the Texas A&M Department of Geography, Facilities and Analytics and Mapping, Marketing and Communications, and Transportation Services.

Aggiemap leads the nation as an example of campus mapping with accessibility features and trip finding capabilities. Aggiemap serves nearly 500,000 visitors per year supporting the 65,000+ students, faculty, and staff that visit Texas A&M everyday.
