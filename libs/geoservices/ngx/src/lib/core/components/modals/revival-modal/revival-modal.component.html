We've been hard at work and are excited to unveil the new and improved version of Texas A&M University Geoservices website!

What's new?

Redesign: The underlying core and technologies remain unchanged. You can expect zero impact in your API or batch processing integrations. This update simply brings a sleek fresh coat of paint to simplify and streamline our product pages. This also means our website is finally responsive and looks great on all devices!

Reworked documentation: We have standardized and re-written all of our service documentation pages to make it easier to explore and discover the meaning behind all of our product output fields.

First-party JavaScript library: Our commitment to make our services easy to use and integrate into other websites and services remains unwavering. If you're a developer, you'll be excited to discover our new first-party JS library compatible with browser and Node environments. Head over to the docs to get started!

What's next?

We will continue to improve and refine this new iteration of the website. If you find any unexpected bugs or issues, please let us know so we can address them.

Thank you for your continued support and we look forward to serving you in the future!

- The Geoservices Team
