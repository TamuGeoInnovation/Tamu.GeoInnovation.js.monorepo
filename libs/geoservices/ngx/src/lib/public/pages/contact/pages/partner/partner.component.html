Request Partner Status

A partner is a user who utilizes the services on this site by being granted special account privileges. Becoming a partner means that you can use our services as many times as you like, for free.

Becoming a partner has a few requirements:

  • You agree that your account information is complete, correct, and up-to-date,
  • You agree that you have completely and truthfully filled out the form below,
  • You agree to provide attribution of our services on your website, products, and/or services that are derived from the use of our services,
  • You agree that you will not disrupt, interfere with, or otherwise abuse, our site, or any services, system resources, accounts, servers, or networks connected to or accessible through the our site,
  • You agree that you will not abuse your partner status in a manner that results in over-use of our site, or any services, system resources, or servers, and
  • You agree that your organization's name and/or logo may be listed on our site.

After you fill out the form you will automatically be allocated 1250 transaction credits while our staff reviews your request.

Please note that our system monitors for user accounts that appear to be abusing their partner status. If a situation arises, the account in question will have its partner status automatically revoked while the matter is investigated.
