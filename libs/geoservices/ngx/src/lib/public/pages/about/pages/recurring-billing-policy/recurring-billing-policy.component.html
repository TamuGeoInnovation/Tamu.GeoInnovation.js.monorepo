Recurring Billing Policy

1. Authorization for Recurring Payments

By accepting this Policy, Customer authorizes Texas A&M GeoServices to charge the credit card or debit the debit card account provided by Customer each month for the balance due on Customer's monthly or yearly Texas A&M GeoServices account.

2. Account Ownership and Sufficient Funds

Customer acknowledges and agrees that the payment card specified for automatic monthly bill payments to Texas A&M GeoServices is, and will continue to be, an account owned by Customer. Customer further undertakes to maintain sufficient availability under the credit card limit or sufficient funds in the account linked to the debit card to cover the monthly charges for the Texas A&M GeoServices account.

3. Timing of Payments

The automatic monthly charge to Customer's credit card or debit to the debit card account will occur on or after the statement due date indicated on the Texas A&M GeoServices account.

4. Recurring Payment Authorization

This Policy shall constitute Customer's written consent and recurring payment authorization to Texas A&M GeoServices. It is advised that Customer retains a copy of this authorization for record-keeping purposes.

5. Cancellation of Recurring Payment Authorization

Customer may cancel the recurring payment authorization by either of the following means: (1) notifying Texas A&M GeoServices through email; or (2) contacting Texas A&M GeoServices via live chat on the Texas A&M GeoServices website. Such cancellation request must be received by Texas A&M GeoServices at least three business days before the statement due date of the relevant month. If the cancellation request is submitted after this deadline, the cancellation will not take effect until the following billing cycle for Customer's Texas A&M GeoServices statement.

6. Effect of Website Unregistration

Customer acknowledges that unregistering from the Texas A&M GeoServices website will not automatically cancel this recurring payment authorization.

7. Contact Information

For any questions or concerns related to this Policy or recurring billing, Customer may contact Texas A&M GeoServices through our contact form.

By accepting this Policy, Customer acknowledges and agrees to be bound by its terms and conditions. Texas A&M GeoServices reserves the right to amend or modify this Policy at any time, and such changes shall be effective upon their posting on the Texas A&M GeoServices website or notification to Customer. It is Customer's responsibility to review this Policy periodically for any updates. Continued use of the recurring payment service after any modifications to this Policy signifies Customer's acceptance of the revised terms.
