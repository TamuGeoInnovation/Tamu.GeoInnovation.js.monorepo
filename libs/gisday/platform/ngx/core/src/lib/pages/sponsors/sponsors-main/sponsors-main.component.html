RASTER

A matrix of cells, also referred to as pixels, arranged into a grid of columns and rows. Each cell represents a value in a variable.

POLYGON

A 2-dimensional, vector, bounded shape composed of a finite sequence of straight line segments called edges or sides, converging at vertices.

LINE

A 2-dimensional vector feature composed of a at least two connected points.

POINT

A 2-dimensional vector feature representing a discrete geographic location.

Booth Setup, Material Storage & Return Shipping

  • Setup: Event sponsors may begin setting up their booths (table cloths, popup signs, etc.) 30 minutes prior to the Career events.
  • Storage: Event sponsors may store their materials at the TxGIS Day Check-In tables which will be located in the MSC outside of each of the GIS Day sessions.
  • Return Shipping: Return shipping is simple. Just bring a return shipping label to affix to your materials, and we will take care of the rest.
