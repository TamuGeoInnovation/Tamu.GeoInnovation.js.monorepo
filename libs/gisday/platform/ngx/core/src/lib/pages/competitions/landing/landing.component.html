VGI Competitions

Each year, TxGIS Day hosts a Volunteered Geographic Information (VGI) data collection competition to engage students, faculty, staff, and other GIS Day participants to get out there and collect some data that is meaningful to the mission of TxGIS Day. The competition has prizes, and allows participants to have an impact on our campus and surrounding community.
