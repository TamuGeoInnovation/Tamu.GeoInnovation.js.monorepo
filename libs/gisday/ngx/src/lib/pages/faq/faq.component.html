Frequently Asked Questions

What you need to know

Should I use my University account username and password to log into the website?

No. The TxGIS Day website is completely separate from Texas A&M's Howdy system. If you have never signed up before on the TxGIS Day website, you should create an account.

Are all of the TxGIS Day events free?

Yes. There is no cost to attend any of the TxGIS Day events.

I am not a student, faculty, or staff member at a university or college - can I come to TxGIS Day?

Yes. We strongly encourage members of the community, colleagues and students from other universities, people from industry, and folks from local, state, and federal agencies to attend.

I am not a student, faculty, or staff member at a university or college - are all the events really free?

Yes. Even if you are not a faculty, staff, or student at a college or university, all TxGIS Day events are free.

Who is allowed to participate in the competitions? Students, Faculty, Staff, Non-Academic people?

  • The presentation and poster competitions are only open to graduate and undergraduate students.
  • The mapping competition is open to everyone who would like to submit data, but only graduate and undergraduate students will be eligible to win.

How do I check-in using the TxGIS Day Website?

You can complete the check-in process for any event using the TxGIS Day website while that event is on-going. Follow these simple steps:

  • Create an account on the TxGIS Day Website.
  • Ensure that you are logged into your account on the TxGIS Day Website.
  • Find the event you are attending and click on it to open its details page.
  • While the event is underway, click on the Check-in button that is visible on the event details page. This will only work during the time period of the event.
  • There will be event volunteers in every session as well as TxGIS Day staff at the check-in tables outside of each session who can assist you with checking in if you need help.

How do I certify my attendance using the GIS Day Website?

You can certify your attendance at any of the TxGIS Day events using the TxGIS Day website. Follow these simple steps:

  • Create an account on the TxGIS Day Website.
  • Make sure that you are logged into your account on the TxGIS Day Website.
  • Open the My Classes menu item on the TxGIS Day website and select all of the classes that you want to notify professors for.
  • While the event it underway, follow the check-in instructions to check-in at the event.

Is there a GIS Day App for Android or iOS?

There are no longer apps for TxGIS Day in the Google Play or Apple App stores. They are no longer needed, since all functions of the site work on mobile devices, including the check-in process and the data collection competition.

If you have previously installed a version of the TxGIS Day app for Android or iOS, you can safely uninstall it.
