RASTER

A matrix of cells, also referred to as pixels, arranged into a grid of columns and rows. Each cell represents a value in a variable.

POLYGON

A 2-dimensional, vector, bounded shape composed of a finite sequence of straight line segments called edges or sides, converging at vertices.

LINE

A 2-dimensional vector feature composed of a at least two connected points.

POINT

A 2-dimensional vector feature representing a discrete geographic location.

Thank you for joining us for Texas A&M University's GIS Day 2020, below is information about each day of the event. The online campus map is an excellent resource for navigating our campus: https://aggiemap.tamu.edu

Monday, November 16

  • The majority of today's events are scheduled at the TAMU Memorial Student Center (MSC) located in the center of TAMU Main Campus at 275 Joe Routt Boulevard, College Station, TX 77840.
  • Plentiful parking is available at any of the TAMU parking garages for GIS Day attendees and guests. The closest parking garage to the MSC is the Center Parking Garage (UCPG).
  • If you are not affiliated with the university you may pick up a 1-day parking pass (each day) from the TAMU GIS Day Check-In tables which will be present in the TAMU MSC outside of each GIS Day session room.
  • Please join us for Happy Hour at Duddley's Draw, located in the North Gate District at 311 University Drive, College Station TX 77840. Paid parking is available in the lot behind the bar.

Booth Setup, Material Storage & Return Shipping

  • Setup: Event sponsors may begin setting up their booths (table cloths, popup signs, etc.) 30 minutes prior to the Career events.
  • Storage: Event sponsors may store their materials at the TAMU GIS Day Check-In tables which will be located in the MSC outside of each of the GIS Day sessions.
  • Return Shipping: Return shipping is simple. Just bring a return shipping label to affix to your materials, and we will take care of the rest.
